Why did you decide to become a web developer?

I remember that, as a child, from the first day that my family got an internet connection, two decades ago, the internet has fascinated me. Most of the things I love about my life, I got using the internet. I spend tons of time in my childhood digging for information about my hobbys in every possible site. Sadly I didn’t really try to build by nyself a proper website up until now. But I do feel that in a way that’s (one of) my porpuse in my life, to do sonething that conects to what so fascinated me in my childhood.
